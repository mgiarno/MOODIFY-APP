Help Center ❓
How does the mood slider work?
Move the slider from 1 (low energy) to 5 (high energy), then tap "Generate Song" — Melo picks a track that matches how you feel and plays it automatically.
How do I use the Pomodoro timer?
Tap the Pomodoro card on the home screen. Hit Start to begin a 25-min study session. Use Pause and Reset anytime. Tap ⛶ for fullscreen mode or "+Set new time" to customize.
Why isn't the music playing?
Make sure the MP3 files are in the same folder as index.html. Tap ▶ on the music screen or use the playlist (☰) to pick a track. Volume must be above 0.
How do I switch songs?
Tap the music card on the home screen to open the player. Use ⏮ ⏭ to skip, or tap ☰ to open the full playlist and pick any song.
What does the AI Study Assistant do?
Chat with Melo about your assignments, exams, or stress. It gives you study plans, schedules, focus tips, and motivation based on what you tell it.
How do I enable Dark Mode?
Go to Settings and tap the "Dark Mood" toggle. The whole app switches to a dark theme instantly. Tap again to switch back to light mode.
